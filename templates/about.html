About Sign Language Learner

Our Mission

Created by STEPHEN KOVIL PILLAI ❤️

Sign Language Learner is dedicated to making sign language education accessible to everyone, especially those with visual or hearing impairments. Our platform provides an inclusive learning environment with specialized tools and features designed to overcome barriers to learning sign language.

Accessibility Features

Enhanced Visibility

Our platform offers text customization with high-contrast color options specifically chosen for visually impaired users, including bright orange, red, and yellow text colors.

Text Size Control

Easily adjust the size of text throughout the application to suit your visual needs with our text resizing controls.

Audio Assistance

Text-to-speech functionality provides audio feedback and guidance for users with visual impairments.

Speech Recognition

Convert spoken words to text and then to sign language, creating multiple pathways for learning and communication.

How It Works

1

Input Text or Speech

Type text directly or use speech recognition to convert your voice to text.

2

Process with NLP

Our natural language processing technology analyzes and processes the text to prepare it for sign language translation.

3

View Sign Language Videos

See clear video demonstrations of each word or phrase in sign language.

4

Practice and Learn

Practice signing through interactive exercises and build your sign language vocabulary and skills.
